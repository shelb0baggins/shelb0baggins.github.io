ABOUT

Hello! I'm Shelby Cuaron. I grew up in a small town in Georgia but quickly grew out of it. I try to wear many hats: dedicated dog mom, Betty Crocker, cool wife, the Office binger, Special Effects Make-Up Artist, Ravenclaw, and finally a digital designer for you!

I found my love for design and for coding at different times. Back in the MySpace days, I used to love manipulating the profile to change colors, make graphics, and have it look exactly how I wanted using code. Design came quite a bit later. I found myself as the yearbook editor at my high school. Graphic Design was a natural passion for me. Now, I turn ideas into reality in the digital space as a front-end developer and UI Designer.
